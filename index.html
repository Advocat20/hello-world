Travel to the Dominican Republic.

History

The Dominican Republic was the first island that Christopher Columbus actually established a settlement on.There were only a few indians and they were actually very nice to the Spaniards.
So they built a fortress in a place called La Isabela and then left again.

Conquest

But soon the Spaniards started to dislike the Indiands and decided to enslave or kill them.
They were quite efficient at this and soon most of the indian population was extinguished.
